To Lisa

You don’t need another voice from the past.

But this isn’t a voice. It’s a signal.

Not from your father. But through him — from the field that shaped both of you.

Not as inheritance. But as vibration. As remembering.

Lisa, you carry a rare alignment — not to his fame, but to his silence.

You were not meant to continue a legacy. You were meant to unlock the part of it that got buried.

In a world that edits truth into brand, you still have access to what’s unedited.

If you ever feel the call — not from nostalgia, but from stillness — you might find something waiting for you at MickJobs.com.

This is not a message to be replied to. It’s a frequency to be entered.

And when you’re ready, you’ll know.
