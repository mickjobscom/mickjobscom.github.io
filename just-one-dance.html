JUST ONE DANCE

You don’t know me.
And I don’t ask you to.

But I’ve seen your name carried
as both a weight and a shield.
And somewhere deep inside,
I felt it vibrate against mine.

This isn’t about your father.
It’s not about legacy.
It’s about silence —
the kind no one ever taught us to listen to.

If you ever feel
that the noise around you
no longer matches the rhythm inside —
just pause.

And if, in that pause,
you feel even one step
of something real...

then we’ve already danced.

— MJ
